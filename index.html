Running Room Crew Management System (RRCMS)

Date: 11th Nov 2019

Project Introduction

Running Room Crew Management System(RRCMS) includes a Kiosk, an Approval Software, and an Admin Dashboard cum Reporting Website.



RRCMS Kiosk

‘RRCMS Kiosk’ is used by Crew to Check-In, Select Room, Select Bed, Select Meal, give Ratings and Reviews to Running Room, and Check-Out.


RRCMS Approval Software

‘RRCMS Approval Software’ is used by Crew Controllers to approve Crew who have signed off on CMS to Check-

In in ‘RRCMS Kiosk’ at Running Room.




RRCMS Admin Dashboard cum Reporting Website

‘RRCMS Admin Dashboard’ can be accessed by concerned administrators, such as Running Room Supervisor, CCC, AME, Sr. DME, etc. from any computer by opening ‘http://rrcms.site’ using Chrome browser.


Running Room Crew Management System(RRCMS) is an Android-based Centralized System that connects Crew Controllers at the lobby and Crew at Running Room over a secured network channel. Crew those who have received approval from Crew Controllers can only stay in Running Room.


This system keeps track of Crew those come into Running Room to rest, automatically, when they Check-In and Check-Out using RRCMS Kiosk, and the corresponding report gets generated in real-time on ‘RRCMS Admin Dashboard Cum Reporting Website’ for the concerned authorities to view. Daily, Monthly, Weekly, Yearly and Custom Datewise reports can also be pulled out from the ‘RRCMS Admin Dashboard Cum Reporting Website’.


Existing Practices

Below are some existing practices carried out by the administration to keep a record of and manage crew which stays in Running Room :

  • No proper verification is done of Crew coming into Running Room, whether he/she has successfully signed off at the lobby or not.

  • Alloting room to Crew is a hectic job for ‘Running Room Supervisor’ as he has to check the Logbook register and alot him a room which is vacant currently, every time a Crew Checks In.

  • Meals given to Crew are noted down as copy of a handwritten Receipt Book. So it becomes tedious to

    follow ‘3 meals in a day + 1 packing per Crew’ rule, each time a Crew asks for a meal.

  • A Logbook register is maintained by ‘Running Room Receptionist’, which is prone to human errors and hence, it’s integrity is not cent percent intact.

  • There is no proper channel to give ‘Rating and Reviews’/Feedback to Running Room.

Above mentioned points are key reasons for inefficient management of Crew at the Running Room. Due to this inefficiency, reports generated by using logbook register are partially correct.


What do we offer?

We offer an Android-based Centralized System called ‘Running Room Crew Management System’ (RRCMS). It is built to simplify the RRCMS Crew management process and generate various reports like Approval Report, Check-In Report, Check-Out Report, Bed Occupancy Report, Meals Report, Rating and Reviews Report, etc.

These reports are cent percent authentic and there is no chance of data manipulation.


Below is a pictorial representation of communication between ‘RRCMS Approval Software’, ‘RRCMS Kiosk’, and ‘RRCMS Admin Dashboard cum Reporting Website’ :


Running Room Crew Management System comprises of mainly 3 parts as follows:


  1. RRCMS Approval Software


    This software is used by Crew Controllers to give approval to Crew who has successfully signed off at the lobby for RRCMS Kiosk at Running Room.


    This is very easy-to-use software. Approval is given in just 2 clicks and takes about 3-4 secs. Up to 10 Crew can be approved simultaneously by a Crew Controller.


    This software is linked with ‘RRCMS Kiosk’ and ‘RRCMS Admin Dashboard cum Reporting Website’. Only Crew approved by this software can Check-In on RRCMS Kiosk at Running Room. Approval given to Crew is also visible on the ‘RRCMS Admin Dashboard cum Reporting Website’ for the administrators to view.


  2. RRCMS Kiosk


    ‘RRCMS Kiosk’ is a highly secured system placed at Running Room Reception. It is linked with the

    ‘RRCMS Approval Software’ and ‘RRCMS Admin Dashboard cum Reporting Website’. ‘RRCMS Kiosk’ is equipped with a Full Touch Display, a Fingerprint Biometric Sensor, a Receipt Printer, a pair of Speakers, and a complex circuitry inside. It is built by keeping in mind about its ease of use. The user’s standing posture while operating RRCMS Kiosk and ergonomics are also taken care of while designing its cabinet. The Fingerprint Sensor adds an added security feature that ensures only authentic person can log in to the RRCMS Kiosk.


    A ‘Running Room Receptionist Admin’ will be created for the Running Room. He/She will supervise the use of ‘RRCMS Kiosk’ by the Crew. He can log in with his admin credentials on ‘RRCMS Kiosk’ and register new Crew members that come into Running Room.


    Crew coming for the first time will have to complete a one-time registration process on the ‘RRCMS Kiosk’ by entering his/her details and enrolling fingerprint. After the one-time registration process, Crew those who have received approval from lobby can log in to the ‘RRCMS Kiosk’ and select Building, Floor, Room, Bed, Meal, and give ‘Review and Ratings’.


    A Crew can only take 3 meals a day and 1 packing at the time of Check-Out as per Railway rule. Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner only show up on ‘RRCMS Kiosk’ during their respective time slots which are 04:00 – 09:30  Breakfast

    09:31 – 16:00  Lunch

    16:01 – 01:00  Dinner


    The crew will have to Check-out on RRCMS Kiosk at the time of leaving Running Room. He/She can take 1 Packing if required before checking out.

  3. RRCMS Admin Dashboard cum Reporting Website

    ‘RRCMS Admin Dashboard cum Reporting Website’ is only for the admins. All different kinds of admins such as Divisional Admin, Lobby Admin, Crew Controller Admin, RR Receptionist Admin, and Guest Admin can log in to this website. All admin roles have different authority to view reports on ‘RRCMS

    Admin Dashboard cum Reporting Website’ according to their designation.

    ‘RRCMS Admin Dashboard cum Reporting Website’ contains reports as follows:

    • Current Approval Report

    • Old Approval Report

    • Current Check-In Report

    • Old Check-In Report

    • Check Out Report

    • Bed Occupancy Report

    • Old Bed Occupancy Report

    • Meals Report

    • Reviews and Rating Report

These reports can be generated by selecting a Date Range(i.e From Date and To Date). Data from the past 2 years can be retrieved from the website by selecting a date range. There is also a Print Report option on the website by which hard copy of the above-mentioned reports can be generated.

Key Benefits

‘Running Room Crew Management System’ keeps track of crew activities in the Running Room, allowing you to make critical operational or maintenance decisions. Below are the key benefits of ‘Running Room Crew Management System’:


  • Authentication – Only authorized person can Check in to Running Room. A Crew need to do successful checkout on CMS and take approval for ‘RRCMS’ from the Crew Controller, then login by using in-built biometric system on ‘RRCMS Kiosk’ in order to a successful Check In.

  • Meal Management – All management authorities can view the amount of food consumed by Crew members, in order to maintain the quality and availability of food products.

  • Room Management – Room allotment is done automatically by the ‘RRCMS Kiosk’. Crew will have

    option to select from vacant rooms only.

  • E-Logbook – An electronic logbook along with other necessary reports will be generated on the website in real-time.

  • Feedback – Crew can give feedback in form of Reviews and Ratings to the Running Room Services via

    ‘RRCMS Kiosk’.

  • Detailed reports – Other detailed reports are also generated e.g. Current Approval Report, Old Approval Report, Current Check-In Report, Old Check-In Report, Check Out Report, Bed Occupancy Report, Old Bed Occupancy Report, Meals Report and Reviews and Rating Report.
